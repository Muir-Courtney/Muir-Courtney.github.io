Weddings & Reception

You've dreamed about this day for years. let us help you plan the perfect wedding day. Along with accommodations, we have a beautiful reception room. We have decorations available to rent in many colors along with centerpieces ready for your personal touches. We have catering options available ranging from an hor d'oeuvres menu to a full service dinner menu.

We have wedding contacts available that have worked with our reception hall previously. These include florists, bakers, photographers, videographers, music coordinator and others. Reviews and recommendations available upon request.

Let us help take the stress out of your wedding day so you can enjoy your special day. Contact us today!
