History

The Manhattan temple was announced on August 7, 2002. The groundbreaking ceremony was held September 23, 2002. The temple was dedicated on June 13, 2004 by Gordon B. Hinckley. The open house was a month long and had 53,000 guests. It is a small temple with the building serving multiple purposes.
