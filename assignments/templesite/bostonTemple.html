History

The Boston Massachusetts Temple was announced on September 30, 1995. The groundbreaking occurred on June 13, 1997. The dedication was held on October 1, 2000 by Gordon B. Hinckley. The open house was attended by 82,600 visitors. The Boston Temple was originally dedicated without a steeple due to a lawsuit by neighbors.
