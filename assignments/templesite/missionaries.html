Full-Time Temple Missionaries

Serving a Full-Time Temple mission is a great blessing. It also comes with some practical needs.

  • Where do you stay?
  • How long will your commute be?
  • What about meals?

Let us be your solution! We offer mini-suites complete with kitchettes for full-time temple missionaries. We have a shuttle to the temple or we offer free parking. Our high speed Internet access allows you to easily keep in touch with family back home. Our pool and exercise room are available for your use. Our family history consultation and ordinance card service allows you to easily work on family history. We offer free grocery delivery. We also have dry cleaning services available for a charge.
