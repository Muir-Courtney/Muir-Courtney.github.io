Welcome

Temples have a very important place in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. We are sealed as families in the temple, we visit to do work for the dead, we renew our covenants, and we serve the Lord in the temple.

We cater to temple workers, patrons, and their families. We have specfic amenties to make your temple visit easier. We do welcome anyone who desires to stay that is willing to abide by our policies and terms.

We continue to build temples. We desire that as many members as possible have an opportunity to attend the temple without having to travel inordinate distances.

--Thomas S. Monson

While we wait for a temple to be an easy day trip for everyone, we at Temple Inn and Suites desire to make your temple day easier. Come visit us today and experience our services for patrons including:

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathrooms
  • Low-cost, short-term babysitting in our playroom
  • Temple History themed reception and sitting area
  • Long-term kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries
  • Reception Hall
  • Family Search Center
  • Temple Shuttle
Whether you need a few hours, a few days, or a few years, we are here for you. Call now to book your stay: 1-800-Temples (836-7537)

Las Vegas Nevada Temple

With spires pointing towards heaven the Las Vegas temple sits on the east side of Las Vegas, away from the Las Vegas Strip. A haven in the city for all who visit the grounds or inside, the Las Vegas temple is a spiritual beacon in "Sin City".
