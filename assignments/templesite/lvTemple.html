History

The Las Vegas Nevada Temple was announced on April 7, 1984. The groundbreaking occurred on November 30, 1985. President Gordon B. Hinckley dedicated the temple on December 16, 1989. The open house was 23 days long and served 297,480 visitors. The family history center was added in 2012.
