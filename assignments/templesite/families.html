For Families

Families have special needs when it comes to temple visits. We can assist with those needs. Wear comfortable clothes in the car. We offer free access to changing rooms and bathrooms. We offer a playroom and we have low-cost, short-term babysitting by trained babysitters. We have a family history center available that includes a printer to print ordinance cards.

To inquire about our services, feel free to contact us.
