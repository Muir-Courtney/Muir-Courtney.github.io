July Weather

by Weather Stalker

It's finally July! And that means it is time to enjoy being outside. It's the nicest weather of the year. Get out there and play baseball, have a barbeque, or just relax and read outside. Around here, we prefer to take our activites outside as much as possible. Homework is outside on the patio. Cooking is outside where it doesn't heat up the house. The firepit is open for business. Time to get the marshmallows, graham crackers, and chocolate out for dessert. Enjoy the time we have outside before winter comes and chases us back inside.
